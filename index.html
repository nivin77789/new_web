APPOINTMENT

OUR PROCESS

Step 1
Conversation

We start with a friendly conversation to understand your loved one’s needs and preferences. If unsure, we can arrange a needs assessment to explore their situation further.

Step 2
Face-to-Face Meeting

We offer a no-obligation visit for a comprehensive needs assessment, including your loved one in the discussion. This allows us to provide initial advice and options tailored to their requirements.

Step 3
Bespoke Package

After our visit, we’ll develop a custom care package that suits your loved one’s specific needs. We’ll also guide you through available funding options to support their care.

Step 4
Caring for Your Family/Friend

If you’re able to assist your loved one, we’ll coordinate with caregivers and healthcare professionals to ensure optimal care. We'll match them with a caregiver who aligns with their needs and interests, giving you peace of mind.
